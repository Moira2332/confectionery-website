Whats Sweet at Beau’s Bonbons

Since she was just a wee child, Lilli dreamt of bringing joy to everyone she met. Those dreams came true in 2012 when she opened Beau’s Bonbons; spreading the sugar high as far and wide as she could. Her infectious enthusiasm quickly won legions of converts, and her store is now the #1 stop for school kids, candy-fiends and everyone on good terms with their sweet tooth.

Lilli gets so excited about all the special treats in her store that she hosts impromptu artisanal candy tastings, mid-day dance parties, and dares school kids to out-pucker each other with sour candies. New discoveries arrive daily––there's always a good reason to stop in and say hello!
